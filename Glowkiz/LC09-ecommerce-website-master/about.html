Founder's Insight

Natasha Gedamkar (Founder & CEO)

In January 2023, Natasha R.G, the visionary behind Glowkiz, embarked on an extraordinary journey. Abandoning her conventional corporate position, she pursued her deep-seated passion for holistic skincare. Armed with a Diploma in Organic Skincare Formulation and a background in crafting remedies for loved ones, Natasha's expertise became the cornerstone of Glowkiz. The birth of Glowkiz was not a mere coincidence; it represented a purposeful move to diminish reliance on synthetic chemicals. The foundational ethos was centered on capturing the pure essence of organic materials abundantly available in nature. At Glowkiz, our mission extends beyond the creation of skincare; we celebrate the beauty of nature and champion the boundless power of compassion. Our meticulously formulated skincare products transcend mere beauty; they serve as a testament to authenticity. From revitalising creams to soothing masks, each product is meticulously designed to nurture your skin's radiance while upholding a commitment to a kinder, more natural world. Glowkiz is not merely a brand; it mirrors our belief in beauty, authenticity, and a sustainable future. With a profound vision of creating a world-changing impact through business profits, Natasha initiated this venture. It's more than just a skincare brand; it's a mission dedicated to uplifting the world.
